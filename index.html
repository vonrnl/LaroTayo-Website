Patintero

Patintero is a traditional Filipino outdoor team game, dating back generations as a popular, low-cost pastime played by children and adults in streets and playgrounds. Derived from the Spanish word tinte ("ink"), it refers to the chalk lines, or sometimes water, used to mark the grid.

Team Play
directions_run

Luksong Baka

Luksong Baka is a traditional Filipino game similar to leapfrog, where players jump over a crouching person (“baka”). It has roots in rural communities of the Philippines, where children played it for fun and physical activity long before modern games became popular.

Jumping
directions_walk directions_run

Ice Ice Water

"Ice Ice Water" (or "Ice Water") is a popular Filipino outdoor chasing game similar to tag, where the "it" player freezes runners by touching them, and they can only be unfrozen by teammates shouting "Water"

About the Game

LARO TAYO

Laro Tayo is a thesis project focuses on developing an educational game that digitally adapts a few traditional street games from the Philippines, specificall targeted at children and students, and is designed for mobile devices. We create this game to keep our traditional filipino street games alive and give the new generation fun and exciting games
