👥 The Team

About Us

We are a group of Filipino students from National College of Science and Technology (NCST) who built Laro Tayo as our thesis project — bringing traditional street games back to life through technology.

Our Mission

Why We Built Laro Tayo

As Filipino students, we grew up hearing stories about street games like Patintero, Luksong Baka, and Langit Lupa — but many kids today have never played them. We wanted to change that.

Laro Tayo is our way of preserving Filipino culture and making it fun and accessible for every elementary student in the Cavite.
