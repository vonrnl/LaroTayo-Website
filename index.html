About the Game

Keeping Filipino Play Alive

Laro Tayo is a thesis project that brings traditional Filipino street games into the hands of today's kids through an Android app. We want every child to know and love the games that shaped our culture.

  • ✅ Teaches Filipino heritage through play
  • ✅ Made for Grades 1 to 6
  • ✅ 4 authentic street games
  • ✅ Fun for the whole class
  • ✅ 100% made by Filipino students
